Stay just a few meters from ZIhuatanejo Bay in the heart of Downtown.

You can enjoy all the charm that this beautiful paradise of the Mexican Pacific offers. Everything at your fingertips so you can enjoy all the wonderful beaches, gastronomy and attractions of Ixtapa Zihuatanejo.


Hotel Zihuatanejo Centro has a great tradition and its 75 rooms offer different comfortable lodging options for all tastes and budgets. Preferred place for stays per day, week or even per month.

With more than 35 years of operation and growth, we are sure that no matter if your trip is for pleasure or business, we will know how to serve you so that your stay is a great experience.

Hotel Zihuatanejo Centro is the emblematic hotel of Zihuatanejo, ideal to enjoy a pleasure trip, fishing, business, in a group or for your unforgettable honeymoon. Preferred by nationals and foreigners. We thank our guests who with more than 20 years of their visit guarantee our personalized service.

Our Services have been designed so that you do not lack anything and only worry about enjoying your stay. Our pool has a wading pool for little tourists. We have 2 restaurant options that will surely be to your liking, where personalized service is provided. In addition, you can enjoy the Spa that will leave you a pleasant experience.
